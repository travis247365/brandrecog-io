FAQ

The questions media buyers ask first.

How is the data captured?

Field teams photograph billboards on any phone. Geo-tagged photos carry GPS and time; the AI recognises the brand and creative, and the site is mapped automatically.

Is this real or a mock-up?

Real. The live demo runs an actual recognition engine on real data for verified business accounts (876 detections across Zambia & South Africa). Free-email sign-ups get the same experience on a mock dataset.

What about audience and traffic numbers?

Foot traffic, vehicle traffic and LSM are available via plug-and-pay providers (Google, footfall sensors, census/LSM data). Every figure is labelled measured, modelled or assumed — we never dress a model as a fact.

Which markets do you cover?

Zambia and South Africa today, with the model built Africa-first. Multi-market is available on Enterprise.

What's the BrandCog.ai roadmap?

OOH is the first channel. Social, radio and TV follow — for a 360° view of Brand Health Tracking (BHT) KPIs across the full media mix.
